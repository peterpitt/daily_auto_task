## Core Announcement

YouTube has begun rolling out a set of updates to its Shorts short-form video product, explicitly designed to align its functionality and user experience more closely with primary competitor TikTok. The changes were announced in an official YouTube support blog post published June 25, 2026.

---

## Key New Features (with TikTok parallels)

### 1. Clear Screen Mode

- Function: Removes all on-screen clutter (icons, text, captions, usernames) from the Short being viewed for an unobstructed viewing experience
- Parallel: Directly mirrors TikTok's long-running *Clear Mode* feature (launched in 2022), which offers identical functionality

> *"Similar to TikTok's 'clear mode,' a new 'clear screen' feature will remove the clutter from the video you're watching."*

### 2. Heart Icon for Likes

- Replaces the existing thumbs-up "like" button with a heart icon, matching TikTok's standard like button design
- Core like functionality remains unchanged

### 3. 2x Speed Playback

- Adds official support for watching Shorts at double speed, with two interaction options:
  - Temporary 2x speed: Hold down the edge of the screen; lift your finger to return to normal playback speed
  - Locked 2x speed: Press the player and swipe down to lock playback at 2x speed

> *"You can now speed up a short by swiping down on your screen."* (accompanied by an official Google demo GIF)

- Parallel: TikTok already offers built-in video speed adjustment for its short-form content

### 4. Mute Shortcut

- Adds a new option to mute Shorts audio directly: tap the video player and select the "mute" button

---

## Removed Feature: Dislike Button

- YouTube is removing the public dislike count button from Shorts entirely
- Official user guidance: Users are encouraged to submit feedback on unwanted content via the three-dots menu, using the *Not interested* and *Don't recommend this channel* options instead
